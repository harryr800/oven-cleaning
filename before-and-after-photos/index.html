Amazing Before and After Photos

Here are some before and after photos of some of the awesome work done by our hard-working team. No Oven is too dirty and we just love to transform your appliance to a near new condition. All of the removable parts are placed in our unique eco-friendly heated soak tank whilst our technician cleans and details the interior of your Oven using eco-friendly solutions. Our technician will also clean in between the glass, behind the backplate where the fan is located and above the top element. The exterior is then degreased and polished to sparkling.

 

Cooktop Before and After

The Cooktop can get very dirty, very easily as it is used a lot. Our technicians will remove the burnt-on carbon from the aluminium burners, trivets, and cooktop surface. Where present we will restore the brass burners to their original shine.

Rangehood Before and After

A Rangehood will not work properly unless it is clean. The filter inside the Rangehood needs to be free from excess grease and fat to enable the motor to extract the cooking oils into the filter. Our technician will remove the filter and cover and clean this at his van and also thoroughly degrease the Rangehood inside and out. Unfortunately, we can not clean inside the motor or inside the chimney.

BBQ Before and After

BBQ’s can be very difficult to clean and return to near their original condition. Our technicians will do their utmost to transform your dirty BBQ to a BBQ that is degreased, cleaned and sanitised. The results may differ for each BBQ due to the age, design and materials the BBQ is made from.
